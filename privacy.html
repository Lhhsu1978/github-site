Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Lido Travel ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through lidotravel.ca and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or deal-alert signup, we collect your name, email address, phone number (optional), and trip preferences such as destination, travel dates, and cabin type. We also use Google Analytics to understand site traffic.

How We Use It

We use this information to respond to your inquiry with a personalized cruise quote, and, only if you opt in, to send you deal alerts and travel offers by email. We do not sell your information to third parties.

Where It's Stored

Form submissions are processed through Web3Forms and logged to a private, internal record for our team's follow-up.

Your Rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete your information, or unsubscribe from our emails at any time. Every marketing email we send includes an unsubscribe link, in accordance with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). To make a request, contact us using the details on our site.

Cookies

We use cookies for basic site analytics. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.
