Am I Licensed?

Yep! Lido Travel operates under Nexion Travel Group Canada, TICO Reg# T1659463 — Ontario's travel regulator. That just means your money's protected and I play by the rules.

Why Book Through Me Instead of Direct?

Same price as booking direct — cruise lines set the fare, I get paid by them, not by charging you more. But you often get extras you won't get on your own: onboard credit, cabin upgrades, sometimes better rates through group bookings. And if anything goes sideways with your trip, you've got one real person (me!) to call instead of a call center.

Why Aren't There More Ships on the Site?

I only post ships I'd actually recommend — newer, well-reviewed, good value. Think of it as skipping the homework of digging through hundreds of options on Expedia. Boutique over big-box.

Can You Book Hotels, Tours, or Flights Too?

Flights — yes, if they're part of your cruise package. Hotels and tours — just message me on WhatsApp with what you're after. Even if I can't score you a better deal, I'll point you somewhere good.
